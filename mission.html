Mission Statement goes here.

Create transformational power and movements by organizing prominent diaspora organizations and members on a dynamic online platform.

Strategic Plan

  1. Start creating an online space (website) for diasporas- taking into account the costs of servers to make sure the site has the capability to house the various organizing needs
  2. Start doing outreach to prominent members of the diaspora and diaspora organizations to see if they would be willing to join a Diaspora Connection coalition.
  3. Once the coalition is formed then Healing Centered Organizing can begin (as diaspora communities require the healing component). The coalition will agree on an initial goal/outcome for their campaign (this does not mean that they cannot do other goals in the future) and execute strategies to achieve their goals.
  4. Will repeat steps one through three with the current diaspora group for additional campaigns and other diasporas that join the platform.
  5. We will also use social media ambassadors online to promote the platform to get individuals to join.
